Service Usage

Pass url you want to shorten in the form /new/<you_url_here> Eg. /new/https://www.google.com

The response will be of the form:

{'original_url': <passed_url>, 'short_url': <generated shorter url>}

Eg. {'original_url': 'https://www.google.com', 'short_url': 'https://murmuring-peak-96105.herokuapp.com/7'}

You can then anytime access your url using the shorter version
